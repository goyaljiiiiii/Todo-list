About the To-Do List Project

This is a simple yet efficient To-Do List application where you can manage your daily tasks based on your needs. The application allows you to add, view, and delete tasks when you have completed them. The tasks are completely under your control, so you can decide whether to keep them or mark them as done and remove them.

Features

  • Task Creation: Easily add tasks with titles and deadlines to keep track of what you need to do.
  • Task Deletion: Once you finish a task, simply delete it from your list to stay organized.
  • Easy Navigation: With smooth scrolling and a user-friendly interface, navigate through your tasks effortlessly.
  • Responsiveness: The app works seamlessly across all devices, whether you're on a desktop, tablet, or phone.

How it Works

After you open the app, you can enter your tasks in the provided fields. You can add a title and set a due date. Once you mark the task as completed, simply delete it to remove it from the list.

The goal of this project is to help you stay organized, keep track of your priorities, and create a sense of accomplishment when tasks are marked as complete!
